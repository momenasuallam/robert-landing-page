The Painful Truth About
Hernia Mesh Implants

To repair a hernia, doctors may implant a woven sheet of mesh to prevent the intestines from bulging through the stomach wall. 

However, manufacturers like Johnson & Johnson failed to warn doctors and patients about the use of toxic synthetics that are not biologically compatible with human tissue and cause:

  •  * Chronic to extreme pain

     * Bowel complications
     * Infections, inflammation, adhesions, and more . . . 

  • Because so many hospitals reported adverse reactions from their patients, a whopping 211,000+ units of hernia mesh implants have been recalled between 2005 and 2019.
